Balmuda Bring your Food Back to Life™

When life gets too busy it's all too easy to forget your leftovers exist and pick up something on the way home from work. But what about the days you just have to catch the premier of your newest reality tv obsession? No worries, just throw those forgotten leftovers in your Balmuda steam powered toaster and cozy up to see what happened this week.

Brief: Get busy singles who let their food go bad to go past the best by date by giving a new life to leftovers.

AD/CW: Rachel Fields and Izzy Hernandez
